## What is this?
This is my portfolio site. It shows my projects, articles, snippets, and small experiments. Everything is arranged neatly so future-me (and curious you) can find the good stuff without tripping over cables.

## Why the cats?
Because the homepage has a giant cat illustration in [src/components/cats.svelte](src/components/cats.svelte). They are not just decoration; they are the unofficial maintainers. If a bug appears, a cat probably already walked across the keyboard to fix it.

## What lives inside
- Projects I made (and the ones I bravely tried to make)
- Articles and notes from my brain vault
- Code snippets I keep reusing
- A guest book for friendly hellos

## Choose your path
